2007 & 2008 years. First iPhone


The main drawback of the first iPhone was absense of AppStore, and as consequense, applications. It is only without 3G supporting.

iPhone was released in 2007 by Apple Inc., first smartphone with volumed sensor screen and operating system iOS.

First generation was not a big hit in humorous expression because of reduced functionality and small sales market. However fishing rod was thrown, and the market was alarmed in earnest.

Company was corrected in iPhone 3G, added 3rd Generation Layer of Network, AppStore and explored geography of sales outside of the USA, including Russia. And then was updated in 2008 with 3Gs model with upgraded camera and processor.

Going back, we have reasons to suppose, that the device itself was the worst in history of iPhones. But it achieved the main - starting massive expansion smartphones all over the world.


2010 year. Release of iPad


Device became the reference in tablet segment, possessed stylish design and opearting system, that was perfect adapted for content consumption.
